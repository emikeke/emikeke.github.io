About Me

Hello I'm Emily, and I'm a Front-end Developer. In my free time, I love spending time with my little dog Coco and my little sister's cat Pumpkin.

Why Coding?

I was fascinated with computers at a young age. It began with playing computer games in 2000 on my dad's computer at work. In 2004, I figured out how to hide the title of the song that was playing on my MySpace page because I didn't want people to steal my playlist. All things added together, coding was always a small hobby I had. I loved learning but I didn't consider it a possible career path at the time.

Career Transition

Fast forward two decades, I was out of school with a Commerce degree and a job that left me slightly unsatisfied. I thought back to my small hobby, but I didn't know how to make it a career. I went to Google for resources. I took multiple online classes and enjoyed it so much. It was then I decided to pursue my small hobby as a full time commitment. I quit my job and started a 12-week coding bootcamp in September of 2021. This reaffirmed my decision to be a front-end developer.
